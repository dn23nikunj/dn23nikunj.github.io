About

A Visionary Bridging Two Worlds

Nikunj Doshi is a Director at Abroad Aashaye — an educational consulting firm empowering Indian students to navigate global study programs. As Director of North America, he has built one of the most trusted support networks for Indians arriving in the United States.

A rare blend of technical mastery and human-centered leadership, Nikunj has guided over 5,000 students through every facet of settling in America — from paperwork and housing to cultural belonging and career growth.

Deeply rooted in Indian heritage while thriving as a Boston community pillar, he embodies the best of both worlds: a DevOps engineer, AI researcher, ambassador, mentor, and seva-driven volunteer who measures success not by titles, but by the lives he touches.

"Most of the Indians always miss their homes outside the country — I have made it my mission to foster communities where they can celebrate festivals with joy and ecstasy."

— Nikunj Doshi
DevOps Engineer AI Researcher Global Ambassador Mentor Community Builder Entrepreneur Social Influencer Public Speaker
