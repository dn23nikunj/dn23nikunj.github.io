About

A Visionary Bridging Two Worlds

Nikunj Doshi is the Director of North America at Abroad Aashaye — an educational consulting firm empowering Indian students to navigate global study programs.

A rare blend of technical mastery and human-centered leadership, he has guided over 5,000 students through every facet of settling in America — from paperwork and housing to cultural belonging and career growth.

Deeply rooted in Indian heritage while thriving as a Boston community pillar, he embodies the best of both worlds: a DevOps engineer, AI researcher, ambassador, mentor, and seva-driven volunteer.

"Most of the Indians always miss their homes outside the country — I made it my mission to foster communities where they celebrate festivals with joy and ecstasy."

— Nikunj Doshi
